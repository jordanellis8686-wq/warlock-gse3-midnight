# Affliction Warlock Rotation Guide - Midnight Patch 12.0.5

## Overview
Affliction Warlock in Midnight focuses on maintaining damage over time effects (DoTs) while managing Soul Shards for burst windows with Summon Darkglare. The spec offers excellent mobility and sustained damage.

## Hero Talent Trees

### Soul Harvester
- **Focus**: Dark Harvest enhancement, burst windows every 1 minute
- **Key Feature**: Dark Harvest regenerates 3 Soul Shards when cast
- **Playstyle**: Aggressive shard spending to reduce Dark Harvest cooldown

### Hellcaller  
- **Focus**: AoE damage with Wither and Malevolence
- **Key Feature**: Corruption replaced by Wither with stacking mechanic
- **Playstyle**: Save shards for Malevolence windows, maintain Wither stacks

## PvE Rotations

### Single Target - Soul Harvester
**Opener:**
1. Precast Haunt
2. Apply Agony
3. Apply Corruption
4. Cast Unstable Affliction
5. Summon Darkglare (with trinkets/potions/racials)
6. Cast Unstable Affliction
7. Dark Harvest
8. Unstable Affliction dump
9. Malefic Grasp/Drain Soul/Shadow Bolt filler

**Sustained Rotation:**
- Maintain Haunt on cooldown
- Keep Agony and Corruption on all targets
- Cast Dark Harvest on CD (don't overcap shards)
- Pool shards for Darkglare windows
- Sync Dark Harvest with Darkglare for shard generation

### Single Target - Hellcaller
**Opener:**
1. Precast Haunt
2. Apply Agony
3. Apply Wither
4. Dark Harvest (before Darkglare - no shard generation for Hellcaller)
5. Summon Darkglare (with trinkets/potions/racials)
6. Malevolence
7. Unstable Affliction dump
8. Malefic Grasp/Drain Soul/Shadow Bolt filler

### AoE/Mythic+ - Soul Harvester
1. Apply Agony to multiple targets while pulling
2. Cast Seed of Corruption
3. Haunt on primary target
4. Spread Agony to 4-6 targets
5. Summon Darkglare (with cooldowns)
6. Dark Harvest
7. Seed of Corruption spam
8. Maintain 4-6 Agonies for shard generation

### AoE/Mythic+ - Hellcaller
1. Apply Agony while pulling
2. Seed of Corruption
3. Haunt on primary target
4. Summon Darkglare
5. Malevolence
6. Seed of Corruption spam with shards
7. Dark Harvest when out of shards
8. Continue Seed spam

## PvP Rotations

### Opener
1. Cast Demonic Gateway from pillar to another map area
2. Place Demonic Circle behind pillar near Gateway
3. Apply Curse of Tongues to casters/healers (if shards available)
4. Apply Curse of Exhaustion to melee (if shards available)
5. Apply Corruption and Agony to all enemies
6. Cast Haunt on primary target
7. Maintain Agony on all targets including pets (generates shards)

### Sustained Damage
1. Maintain curse usage based on target type
2. Keep Corruption on all targets
3. Keep Agony on all targets (never let fall off - stacks to 18)
4. Haunt on kill target
5. Unstable Affliction on kill target until out of shards
6. Summon Darkglare
7. Dark Harvest
8. Shadow Bolt with Nightfall procs
9. Shadow Bolt as filler

### Burst Damage Window
- Focus single target while maintaining DoTs on others
- Use all cooldowns together
- Dump shards with Unstable Affliction
- Follow with Dark Harvest
- Use Nightfall procs immediately

## DoT Pandemic Windows
- **Agony**: 4.5 seconds
- **Corruption**: 4.2 seconds  
- **Unstable Affliction**: 6.3 seconds
- **Wither**: 4.2 seconds (Hellcaller)

## Stat Priorities

### PvE Single Target
**Crit > Haste = Mastery > Versatility**

### PvE AoE/Mythic+
**Haste = Crit > Mastery > Versatility**
- Haste becomes more valuable for shard generation and Seed casts

### PvP
**Intellect > Versatility > Haste > Critical Strike > Mastery**
- Versatility provides damage increase and damage reduction
- Haste reduces GCD and increases DoT tick rate

## Key Mechanics

### Soul Shard Generation
- **Agony**: Primary shard generator (chance per tick)
- **Nightfall**: Proc for free Shadow Bolt (max 2 stacks)
- **Shard Instability**: Proc for bonus damage (max 2 stacks)

### Dark Harvest Usage
- **Soul Harvester**: Cast on CD, generates 3 shards
- **Hellcaller**: Cast before Darkglare, no shard generation
- Don't overcap shards before casting

### Darkglare Windows
- Pool 3-5 shards before casting
- Use all offensive cooldowns together
- Dump shards rapidly during window
- Follow with Dark Harvest for shard regeneration (Soul Harvester)

## Defensive Cooldowns
- **Unending Resolve**: 40% damage reduction
- **Dark Pact**: Sacrifice health for shield
- **Mortal Coil**: Fear and heal
- **Demonic Healthstone**: Enhanced healthstone with Soulburn
- **Demonic Circle: Teleport**: Mobility tool

## Utility
- **Curse of Tongues**: Increases cast time for casters/healers
- **Curse of Exhaustion**: Slows melee targets
- **Curse of Weakness**: Situational damage reduction
- **Create Soulwell**: Healthstones for team
- **Demonic Gateway**: Team mobility

## Tips and Optimization
- Refresh DoTs while moving (better than nothing)
- Never let Agony fall off in PvP (maintains 18 stacks)
- Use Drain Soul for execute damage and shard sniping
- Maintain Haunt uptime for Apex Talent procs
- Sim your character for precise stat weights
- Use raidbots.com Top Gear for gear optimization
